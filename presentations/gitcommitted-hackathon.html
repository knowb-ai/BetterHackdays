GitCommitted

Match vibe coders by stack, energy, and goals. Build together at hackathons, meetups, and on the street.

Problem

Great builders sit alone in the crowd.

  • You show up to a hackathon or event with ideas, but no teammate who gets your vibe.
  • Skill lists on Discord do not capture how you work, what you care about, or what you want to ship.
  • Random pairing wastes the first 6 hours on misaligned goals.

Who feels it

Vibe coders everywhere.

  • Hackathon hackers who want a co-builder, not a resume collector
  • Indie founders at business mixers looking for a technical partner
  • Remote devs who want a real work environment match, not just a job title
  • Cursor / AI builders shipping fast and needing someone on the same wavelength

Solution

GitCommitted matches you before you commit.

A lightweight profile plus vibe signals. We surface people with similar interests, stack, and build style for events and everyday collab.

How it works

Three steps. Under 2 minutes.

01

Set your vibe

Stack, role, energy level, what you want to build this weekend.

02

Scan a QR at the event or open the hackathon room code.

03

Match and ship

See top matches, ping them, form a team before pizza arrives.
