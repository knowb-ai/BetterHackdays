GitCommitted street survey

~3 min · Interviewer fills in · Target: builders, students, tech-curious strangers

Opener: "Hi, we are testing a free app that helps coders and builders find teammates with a similar vibe at hackathons and events. Got 3 minutes? We will not ask for your email unless you want early access."

Q1 · Screener

Do you write code, design products, or build things with tech (even as a hobby)?

Single choice

Q2

Have you ever gone to a hackathon, dev meetup, or builder event?

Single choice

Q3 · Problem

When you need a teammate or collaborator, what is hardest?

Pick up to 2

Q4

How do you usually find people to build with today?

Multiple choice

Q5 · Concept

GitCommitted matches you to builders with similar stack, goals, and "vibe" before or during an event. How appealing is that?

Scale 1–5 (1 = not at all, 5 = very)

Q6

Where would you actually use something like this?

Multiple choice

Q7

What would make you trust a match from an app?

Open response

Q8 · Close

Would you try a 60-second profile if we sent you a link after the hackathon?

Single choice

Interviewer notes

Time target: 3 minutes. Skip Q4 if they are in a hurry.

Record: Age range (18–24 / 25–34 / 35+), gender (optional), role (dev / design / biz / student).
